Chicken Curry

Description

This is a delicious chicken curry recipe that is easy to make and packed with flavor. Perfect for a family dinner or a cozy night in.

ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

    • Steps

    1. Cook the basmati rice according to package instructions.
    2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
    3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until translucent.
    4. Add the diced chicken breast and cook until browned.
    5. Stir in the curry powder and cook for another minute.
    6. Pour in the coconut milk, bring to a simmer, and cook for 15 minutes.
    7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    8. Serve the curry over the cooked basmati rice and garnish with fresh cilantro.
