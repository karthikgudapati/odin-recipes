Pasta

Description

This is a simple and delicious pasta recipe that can be made in under 30 minutes. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

    • Steps

    1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    3. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
    4. Stir in the diced tomatoes and oregano, and let simmer for 10 minutes.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Drain the spaghetti and toss it with the sauce.
    7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
