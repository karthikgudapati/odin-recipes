Protein Shake

Description

This protein shake is a quick and nutritious option for breakfast or post-workout recovery. Packed with protein and healthy fats, it's perfect for anyone looking to boost their energy levels.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop protein powder (whey or plant-based)
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 250ml almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

    • Steps

    1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
    2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
    3. Taste and adjust sweetness with honey if desired.
    4. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
